FEB

Somalia Drought Relief

Organized By: NewDawn Charity Foundation

Time: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Location: Shamo Hotel Conference Hall

Phone: 0803-445-6778

Email: info@newdawncharity.org

The Somalia Drought Relief event raised awareness and support for communities affected by severe drought and food shortages. It brought together donors, volunteers, and organizations to inspire action, share impactful stories, and mobilize resources for food, water, and essential aid.

event conclusion

  • The event successfully raised awareness about the severity of the drought crisis in Somalia.
  • Generous donations and pledges were received to support ongoing relief efforts.
  • Strong partnerships were formed between organizations, volunteers, and donors.
  • Participants left inspired and committed to continuing support for affected communities.

Our events focus on providing support through education, healthcare, and emergency relief for communities affected by disasters and conflict.

You can join by registering online, volunteering, donating, or participating in both physical and virtual events.

Some events are free, while others may require a registration fee or donation. Details are always provided on each event page.

Yes, we welcome volunteers! You can sign up through the volunteer section or indicate your interest during event registration.

Donations can be made during the event through cash, bank transfer, or digital payment options. You’ll find clearly marked donation points and support staff available to assist you.

All donations support our programs in education, healthcare, disaster relief, and assistance for vulnerable communities.

You can expect engaging activities, impact stories, opportunities to give, and a chance to connect with others supporting the cause.

Each event helps provide essential resources like food, medical care, education, and hope to those who need it most.
