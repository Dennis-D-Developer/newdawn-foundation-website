Why Should You Care?

Every day, thousands of children around the world face a reality they did not choose.

  • Over 3,600 children under the age of 5 die every single day from preventable causes.
  • Undernutrition remains one of the leading killers, responsible for nearly half of these deaths.
  • The most fragile time is the first month of life. More than half of child deaths happen within the first 28 days.
  • Millions of children are growing up stunted, underdeveloped, and without access to proper nutrition.
  • Education is slipping out of reach. Millions of children are out of school, while many who attend struggle with basic reading and math.

This isn’t just one country’s crisis, it’s global. From Mozambique to Nigeria, Syria to Sudan, Kenya to Haiti, and even regions affected by the Russia–Ukraine conflict and Iran, children are facing hunger, displacement, and lost futures. At NewDawn Charity Foundation, we work every day to change these outcomes, to give children a chance not just to survive, but to thrive.

How You Can Help

Change doesn’t require millions, it starts with one decision. By contributing even a small portion of your income, you can:

  • Provide life-saving nutrition and healthcare.
  • Support education for out-of-school children.
  • Deliver emergency relief in crisis regions.
  • Help rebuild futures in communities affected by conflict and disaster.

As part of our commitment to transparency, you’ll receive:

  • Regular updates on the impact you’re making.
  • A clear view of how your support is transforming lives.
